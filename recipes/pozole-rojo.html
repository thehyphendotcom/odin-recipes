Pozole Rojo

Description

Pozole Rojo is a traditional Mexican soup made with hominy, meat (usually pork), and a rich red chili broth. It's often garnished with shredded lettuce, radishes, onions, lime, and oregano.

Ingredients

  • Hominy
  • Pork shoulder
  • Red chili peppers
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Lime juice
  • Cilantro

Steps

  1. Cook the pork shoulder until tender, then shred it.
  2. Prepare the red chili broth by blending the red chili peppers with onions and garlic, then simmering it with the shredded pork.
  3. Add the hominy to the broth and cook until heated through.
  4. Serve the pozole in bowls and garnish with shredded lettuce, radishes, onions, lime wedges, and oregano.
