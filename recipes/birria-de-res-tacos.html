Birria de Res Tacos

Description

Birria de Res Tacos are a delicious Mexican dish made with slow-cooked beef (res) that is marinated in a flavorful blend of spices and chilies. The beef is cooked until tender and then shredded, making it perfect for filling tacos. These tacos are often served with a side of consommé, which is a rich broth made from the cooking juices of the beef, allowing you to dip the tacos for added flavor.

Ingredients

  • Beef (res) - typically chuck roast or brisket
  • Dried chilies (such as guajillo, ancho, and pasilla)
  • Garlic
  • Onions
  • Spices (such as cumin, oregano, and bay leaves)
  • Vinegar
  • Salt and pepper
  • Corn tortillas
  • Optional toppings: chopped onions, cilantro, lime wedges, and shredded cheese

Steps

  1. Marinate the beef with a blend of dried chilies, garlic, onions, spices, vinegar, salt, and pepper. Let it sit for at least a few hours or overnight for best results.
  2. Slow cook the marinated beef until it is tender and can be easily shredded. This can be done in a slow cooker, oven, or on the stovetop.
  3. Once the beef is cooked, shred it using two forks and set it aside.
  4. Warm the corn tortillas and fill them with the shredded beef.
  5. Serve the tacos with a side of consommé for dipping, and garnish with optional toppings like chopped onions, cilantro, lime wedges, and shredded cheese.
